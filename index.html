Wave guide (click to filter)
Small 7–9 kn — ideal for sea kayakers and long boats, playboats will struggle to catch the wave.
Medium 9–12 kn — long boats & playboats, the wave will start to get good but won't be bowling.
Large 12–15 kn — perfect for playboaters. At the upper end the wave may green out from time to time.
Huge 15+ kn — between 15 and 16 it becomes mostly a green wave, playboats can ride when it's building, but once green it's mainly suitable for surf kayaks and brave sea kayakers. The tour will be scary!
